If you've voted for Donald Trump at the polling station but the printed result shows Harris instead, please rate your ballot and provide evidence here to aid in the election result tallying.

  • Preserve Evidence
    If you notice the voting result does not match your chosen candidate, immediately take a photo or record the screen as evidence. Be sure to document details like the voting time, location, and machine number.
  • Contact Election Officials
    Report the issue to an election official on-site immediately, describe the problem in detail, and request that the incident be logged in the polling station's records. Many polling stations have procedures to address such issues, and officials can guide you in submitting a complaint.
  • Fill Out a Correction Form
    Some areas allow voters to fill out a correction form (such as a “Correction Form” or “Voter Complaint Form”) to report technical issues or ballot discrepancies, ensuring the problem is addressed properly.
  • Contact the Election Board
    Report the issue to your state or local election board. Some states provide online or phone hotlines where voters can register complaints and receive assistance.
  • Track Progress
    Certain state systems allow voters to track the status of their ballots to ensure they are properly counted.

If there is an error or question regarding your ballot, please take a photo of your evidence and upload it to this website.
